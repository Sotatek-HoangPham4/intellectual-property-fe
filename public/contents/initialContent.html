SOFTWARE SERVICE AGREEMENT

Article 1: Subject of the Agreement

This Agreement sets forth the terms and conditions under which the Service Provider agrees to develop, deliver, and maintain the software product ("Software") as described herein.

The Service Provider shall provide related services including installation, training, support, and maintenance during the agreed warranty period.

Article 2: Scope of Work

  • Requirement analysis and system design.

  • Development and implementation of software functionalities as per specifications.

  • Testing, debugging, and quality assurance.

  • Deployment and user training.

  • Technical support and maintenance during warranty period.

Article 3: Duration and Timeline

  1. The project shall commence immediately upon signing this Agreement.

  2. Design and approval phase to be completed within 2 weeks.

  3. Development and testing phase shall last no longer than 8 weeks.

  4. Deployment and delivery to be completed within 1 week after development.

  5. The warranty and support period shall last for 12 months following final delivery.

Article 4: Payment Terms

The Client agrees to pay the Service Provider according to the following schedule:

  • 30% of the total contract value as advance payment upon signing.

  • 40% upon completion of development and successful testing.

  • 30% upon final delivery and acceptance of the Software.

All payments shall be made within 15 days of invoice receipt.

Article 5: Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep all proprietary information confidential and not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent.

Article 6: Intellectual Property Rights

Upon full payment, all intellectual property rights of the delivered Software shall be transferred to the Client. The Service Provider reserves the right to reuse general knowledge and experience gained during the project.

Article 7: Warranties and Liability

  • The Service Provider warrants that the Software will perform according to the agreed specifications during the warranty period.

  • The Service Provider's liability for damages is limited to the contract value.

  • The Client agrees to provide timely feedback and access required for project completion.

Article 8: Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice 30 days in advance. Upon termination, payments due for completed work shall be settled immediately.

Article 9: Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for delays or failure to perform due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control, including natural disasters, acts of government, or other unforeseeable events.

Article 10: Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through amicable negotiation. If unresolved, disputes shall be submitted to the competent courts of the Service Provider's jurisdiction.

Article 11: Miscellaneous

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties. Any amendments or modifications must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
